About this Project

Poems Around Galway is an educational project to bring poetry written about Galway city to anyone. We've selected some of the most iconic poems about this city for readers to read, listen, and enjoy. This is a way for locals to find their favourite poems or learn about writing they haven't seen before. It's a great resource for educators! This site is also for visitors to the city to get to know this poetic city and check out local bookshops.

Galway is the 4th largest city on the island of Ireland and has a unique landscape. The city is built on a weir, which helps mitigate the large volume of rain that falls annually. The canal created by the weir is actually quite romantic (in nice weather). Galway is also a medieval port city built along Galway Bay and protected from the Atlantic ocean. This city's location, geography, and weather greatly influences people born in the city, and visitors.
